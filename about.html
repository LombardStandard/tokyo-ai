About Tokyo AI

We started building out the TAI community on November 2nd, 2023, through Tokyo AI Talks (TAIT), an invite-only community that met every two months over three hours for a series of lightning talks and panels, with the objective of connecting the brightest engineers, scientists, entrepreneurs, and investors interested in knowledge sharing, serendipity, and engineering excellence. Among the speakers, we had CTOs of Tokyo-based startups, researchers from top universities, software architects from established tech companies, and other profiles able to contribute a high level of discussion.

Today TAI is a vibrant community passionate about the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning progress, working together to make Tokyo a global AI hub.

Vision & Mission

Transforming Tokyo into the world's leading AI innovation hub, driving global technological advancement.

Tokyo AI is a catalyst for technological innovation, dedicated to establishing Tokyo as a global AI powerhouse. We build a dynamic, collaborative AI ecosystem by fostering meaningful connections and facilitating knowledge exchange. Through targeted meetups and events in both English and Japanese, we bridge Japanese and international AI communities, creating a unique platform for collaboration. More than just a network, our community is an action-driven collective of entrepreneurs, researchers, and builders committed to advancing AI.

Values

Our values define our work, setting the standard for our community’s culture and impact.

  • Inclusivity, diversity, and respect – AI should be accessible, and as many people as possible should benefit from its advancements.
  • Collaboration-first mindset – Achieving our vision requires the collective effort of the entire community.
  • Excellence in content – We prioritize depth and quality, avoiding superficial discussions or debates for the sake of argument.

Ecosystem

TAI is designed to bring exceptional minds together, fostering collaborations that drive innovation. We’re not just observing the future of AI: we’re actively building it.

Our goal is to elevate Japan’s tech and startup landscape to global prominence, ensuring Tokyo becomes a vital node in the worldwide AI ecosystem.

What Sets Us Apart

  • International perspective, locally rooted
  • A diverse mix of talent from academia, startups, and industry
  • Multilingual engagement (English and Japanese)
  • A strong bias for action and technological progress
