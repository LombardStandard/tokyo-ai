TokyoAI

Transforming Tokyo into a Global AI hub

Tokyo AI (TAI) is the largest AI community in Japan, bringing together over 2,000 members, engineers, researchers, investors, product managers, and corporate innovation leaders, primarily based in Tokyo. We are a hub for cutting-edge AI discussions, enabling collaboration between startups, academia, and industry to drive meaningful technological advancements.

Interest Groups

Applied ML and AI

Workshops and meetups focused on AI and ML applications. AMA is responsible for making sure the Tokyo AI community is informed on the latest developments in AI and ML, specifically through a more applied and hands-on approach.

Applied ML and AI in Japanese

AMAJ is the Japanese version of AMA. We want to see a more global community in Japan, but we also realize that a lot of research here is being done in Japanese, so some members are more comfortable sharing their knowledge in the local language.

Advanced AI

More advanced discussions on AI from first principles. More academic, with topics like Mathematics, Physics, Information Theory, Statistical Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. Focused on the foundational research, making the future of AI.

AI in Hardware, and Robotics

AI and hardware are already intertwined, given the strong use of hardware accelerators in AI model training and inference. When combining AI, Hardware, and Robotics, we see Japan as the top place for engineering and entrepreneurship.

AI Salon Tokyo

AI Salon is a global community by and for AI-focused founders, builders, investors, and enterprise partners. The format is a monthly in-person event with lightning talks from startups with a presence in Japan, showing the products they are working on to other builders, partners, and investors. We sometimes have also foreign AI startups visiting Japan, as we want to be their lending pad in Tokyo. AI Salon has been launched in Austin (US) by Jeffrey Abbott, GP at Blitzscaling Ventures, who decided to partner for their Tokyo chapter with TAI through the JETRO patronage and DEEPCORE support.

Tokyo AI Talks

TAIT is our invite-only community, with limited seats available for each of the bi-monthly sessions. The value of TAIT is not only in the topics discussed but even more so in the network and in the interactions during the sessions.
